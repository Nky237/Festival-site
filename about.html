About

The Ancient city is a brand, a new kind of brand we build together preserving our heritage. A brand for the Metaverse. By the community. A first collection of 5,000 Gatekeepers that will give you membership access to the Ancient city. Heritage is our gift from the past, what we live with today, and what we pass on to future generations. Now, the line between the physical and digital worlds are blurring and the rules are being rewritten. We must carry our culture, heritage and history to the future. The Ancient city is a corner of the internet, an archive in the Metaverse where art, community and culture fuse to create magic. To enter the city, one must be proven worthy and ready to acquire the higher knowledge and insights.
